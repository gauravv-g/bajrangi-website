03Voice
Brand Voice
Direct. Witty. Bharosemand. No emotional softness.
The Personality
Direct. Witty. Bharosemand.

Not corporate. Not humble. Not emotional. The confidence of a person who knows their product is better and says so — without apology, without explaining themselves, without asking for approval.

Every line should sound like it could be the last line of a 30-second ad film. That's the test.
Use These Words
  • भरोसा — not "trust" or "reliability"
  • मतलब — the brand owns this word
  • गिन के देखो — the challenge, everywhere
  • पूरा माल, पक्का भरोसा — the promise
  • पंगत — real word for a feast row
  • भंडारा / शादी / प्रसाद — real occasion names
  • ठेला / ढाबा / हलवाई — real buyer names
  • सिर्फ बजरंगी में — exclusivity claim
Never Say
  • "Premium quality" without proof
  • "Industry leading" anything
  • "We are delighted to offer"
  • Emotional / sentimental language
  • Any corporate English jargon
  • Any stat you cannot verify face-to-face
